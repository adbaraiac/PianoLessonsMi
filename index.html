Frequently Asked Questions

How much are lessons?

Weekly 30-minute lessons are $35 per lesson. We’ll confirm your teacher and schedule during signup.

Yes! We travel to homes in Troy, Rochester, and nearby communities.

What ages do you teach?

We teach kids, teens, and adult beginners. We tailor the pace and songs to each student.

A keyboard is completely fine, We can also help you choose one that fits your space and budget.
